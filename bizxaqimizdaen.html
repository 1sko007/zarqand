    About us

  • Zarqand is a confectionery manufacturer in Uzbekistan, operating since 2006.

    The company employs over 250 people. Manufacturing is located in Tashkent. The peculiar nature of production and its scale allows the company to have an individual variety of products among domestic producers.

    Today the assortment includes over 40 kinds of unique confectionery products.

Our Company

Zarqand is not only a confectionery manufacturer, but also an FMCG goods distributor.

  • Distribution activities of the company started in 2013 and now sells throughout Uzbekistan via 15 branches in major cities of the country. All branches of the company operate in a single software, which allows to manage the team as efficiently as possible.
    Today, Zarqand distribution team is the strongest team of over 100 professional employees.

    Company’s portfolio includes brands TM “ZARQAND” and TM “CO BARRE DE CHOCOLATE” of the company “Vash Shokolatie+”.

    ZARQAND as a distributor is welcome to new offers for cooperation.
